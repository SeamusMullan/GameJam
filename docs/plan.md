# General Plan

## Game name: Spacebar: Mania

## Genre of game: Wave based customer game (Overcooked)

## Overview of gameplay

- On spaceship, always on the move, everything is all over the place.
- Overcooked-like game, customers get angry over time.
- Tips from customers for vending machine.
- Dodgy vending machine for agartha white monster :D
- Pour yourself a pint, get a status effect or something
- Waves of customers at the bar.
- Wave based upgades.

### Items

- Crisp (Tayto) Sandwich
- Flat 7ip
- Pint pouring speed modifiers
- Cocktail speed modifiers

### Upgrades

- Drink on the job
