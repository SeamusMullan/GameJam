# Schedule & Milestones

This is a sample schedule for a 48-hour jam. Adjust times to match your jam window.

- **Hour 0:** Kickoff — ideas, vote, team formation
- **Hours 1–6:** Rapid prototyping and core mechanics
- **Hours 6–24:** Core systems and basic art
- **Hours 24–36:** Content, levels, and sound
- **Hours 36–44:** Polish, bugfixing, and testing
- **Hours 44–48+:** Build, package, and submit

Tip: Break the team into small focused tasks (programming, art, audio, QA).
